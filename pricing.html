Common questions

A few things worth knowing.

What if my company has more than 1,500 employees? +

Above 1,500 employees the consulting workload genuinely shifts — more entities, more pay structures, more board reporting. We'll build a custom Enterprise+ engagement that reflects that scale. Talk to us; we'll give you a real number, not a "request a quote" page.

Who's the senior advisor? +

Stuart Reeves on most engagements — 25+ years of compensation work across Fortune 500 and mid-market companies. As CompIQ scales, additional senior advisors will join the practice, all hand-selected from a vetted advisory network. You'll never be passed to a junior analyst.

How does the Salary.com partnership work? +

CompIQ has a partnership with Salary.com that brings enterprise-grade market data directly into the platform. You don't license Salary.com separately — it's included in every tier. Core refreshes quarterly; Intelligence and Enterprise refresh monthly.

What does onboarding actually look like? +

We ask for an employee roster (one spreadsheet, common fields). We map your roles to the Salary.com job library, validate the data against your benchmarks, and schedule a kickoff with your senior advisor. Most engagements go live in two to three weeks.

Monthly or annual — which should I pick? +

Monthly if you want to test the thesis quickly with no long-term commitment. Annual if you're confident — you get a meaningful discount on every tier, and onboarding is included rather than billed separately. Most customers start monthly and convert to annual within their first two quarters.

Can I cancel? +

Monthly plans cancel any time. Annual plans cancel at the end of the term — we'll never auto-renew without confirming with you first.
