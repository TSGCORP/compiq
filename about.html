About

Built by a consultant
who got tired of watching the same mistakes.

CompIQ exists because mid-market HR leaders have been making the same compensation mistakes for thirty years — not because they're bad at their jobs, but because they've been forced to use tools that don't match their reality.

"I sat across the table from a CHRO at a $400M company who'd just lost three of her best engineers in six weeks. She had Payscale. She had a comp committee. She had a deck from a Big Four consultant from 18 months ago. What she didn't have was anyone telling her what to do about it."

— Stuart Reeves, Founder

That conversation happened in early 2024. By the end of the year, the rough shape of CompIQ existed on a whiteboard. By 2025, it was a working dashboard with real clients. By 2026, it was a partnership with Salary.com — the largest provider of compensation data in North America.

The thesis hasn't changed: mid-market HR leaders deserve the same caliber of compensation thinking that Fortune 500 companies pay Mercer two hundred thousand dollars to access. They don't deserve software that pretends consulting isn't part of the equation. They deserve both — at a price that fits the size of the company they actually run.

Founder & Lead Consultant
Stuart Reeves
The founder

Stuart spent 25 years inside compensation.

He started his career in compensation consulting at a Fortune 500 staffing firm, where he led merit cycle planning and pay structure design for clients across financial services, healthcare, and professional services. He's seen comp from inside the corporate boardroom and from across the table as a consultant.

For the past several years, he's worked as an independent compensation advisor to mid-market and growth-stage employers — the same companies that couldn't justify the cost of Mercer or Aon but needed real senior thinking. CompIQ is the company he built to bring that caliber of advisory to a much bigger market — pairing it with a software platform so it scales without losing the judgment layer that makes it valuable.

What we believe

Five things that guide every decision we make.

01

Judgment is the product.

Software displays data. Consultants render judgment. CompIQ is both — and the judgment is the part that actually changes outcomes.

02

The middle deserves better.

Mid-market HR leaders run real businesses with real complexity. They shouldn't have to choose between enterprise prices and toy software.

03

Pricing should be public.

If we can't tell you what something costs without a discovery call, we're not actually ready to sell it to you.

04

Data without a point of view is noise.

Every CompIQ dashboard is designed to answer a specific question. If a chart isn't earning its real estate, it doesn't ship.

05

Tell clients hard truths.

The most valuable thing a consultant can do is say the thing nobody else in the room is willing to say. We don't soften our quarterly reviews to be polite.

—

Your turn to push back.

We won't agree with every comp decision your team has made. That's the value, not the bug.
